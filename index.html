Scaling engagement
from zero to 60%

Managing social media campaigns while simultaneously moderating harmful content is a high-skill balancing act. At Bhanzu, the challenge was not just creating engaging content — it was doing so at speed while ensuring full brand-safety compliance and measurable audience growth.

By integrating moderation and screening directly into the content production workflow, both engagement quality and volume improved significantly.

The Challenge

Maintaining rapid content output across multiple platforms while removing brand-unsafe material without slowing down campaigns or audience growth.

The Objective

Build a streamlined content creation and moderation system delivering consistent engagement growth while protecting brand reputation at scale.

Process

Analysed existing content performance and audience sentiment via Sprinklr and Sprout Social.
02

Case Study · 02

Multi-market campaigns —
Dubai & beyond

At Rynock Solutions in Dubai, managing digital campaigns across diverse cultural markets required deep understanding of audience expectations, platform nuances, and GCC compliance standards.

Approach

Built a content system anchored in analytics. Influencer selection driven by audience overlap data, not follower count. Ownership of Meta Ads budgets up to ₹1.5L/month with systematic A/B testing.

Key Discoveries
  • GCC audiences engaged best with short-form video over static posts
  • SEO-optimised WordPress content doubled organic search visibility within 3 months
  • Influencer authenticity outperformed reach as a predictor of conversion rate
  • Brand consistency across platforms was the top driver of audience loyalty growth
  • Weekly analytics reviews allowed rapid strategy pivots maintaining campaign momentum
