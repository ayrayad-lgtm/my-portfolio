Case Study · 01

Scaling engagement
from zero to 60%

Managing social media campaigns while simultaneously moderating harmful content is a high-skill balancing act. At Bhanzu and across earlier roles, the challenge was not just creating engaging content — it was doing so at speed while ensuring full brand-safety compliance and measurable audience growth.

By integrating moderation and screening directly into the content production workflow — rather than treating it as an afterthought — both engagement quality and volume improved significantly.

The Challenge

Maintaining rapid content output across multiple platforms while removing hate speech, abusive content, and brand-unsafe material without slowing down campaigns or audience growth.

The Objective

Build a streamlined content creation and moderation system that delivers consistent engagement growth, protects brand reputation, and scales efficiently with minimal escalation incidents.

Case Study · 01 · Process

Plan → Create
→ Moderate → Optimise

01
Content Audit
Analysed existing content performance, audience sentiment, and engagement patterns using Sprinklr and Sprout Social. Identified gaps and high-performing content types.
02
Strategy & Calendar
Developed a platform-specific content calendar integrating moderation checkpoints. Defined content pillars, brand voice, and escalation protocols for sensitive content.
03
Design & Creation
Crafted original graphics using Photoshop and Illustrator. Wrote captions and copy tailored to each platform's audience. Video content edited in DaVinci Resolve.
04
Analyse & Iterate
Used Google Analytics and platform insights to track performance. Conducted sentiment analysis post-campaign and fed learnings back into the next cycle's strategy.

The tight integration of content creation and moderation — rather than siloed workflows — reduced review time by removing back-and-forth while improving brand safety outcomes consistently.

Case Study · 02

Multi-market campaigns
— Dubai & beyond

At Rynock Solutions in Dubai, managing digital campaigns across diverse cultural markets required more than strong design — it demanded deep understanding of audience expectations, platform nuances, and compliance across GCC market standards.

From influencer partnerships to SEO-driven WordPress management, every touchpoint needed to be cohesive, performance-driven, and culturally attuned.

Approach

Built a content system anchored in analytics — using data from previous posts to inform visual style, caption tone, and posting schedules. Influencer selection was driven by audience overlap data, not just follower count.

Key Discoveries
  • Platform behaviour varies significantly by market — GCC audiences engaged best with short-form video over static posts
  • SEO-optimised WordPress content doubled organic search visibility within 3 months
  • Influencer authenticity outperformed reach as a predictor of conversion rate
  • Brand messaging consistency across all platforms was the top driver of audience loyalty growth
  • Weekly analytics reviews allowed rapid strategy pivots that maintained campaign momentum
